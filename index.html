Our Story

Born in 2012 from a love of good coffee and Mumbai's vibrant arts scene, Kala Ghoda Café has grown into a beloved institution. What began as a modest coffee shop is now a cultural landmark where creatives, professionals, and coffee lovers gather.

We're passionate about every detail - from sourcing beans directly from ethical farms to crafting pastries in-house each morning. Our space evolves with rotating art exhibitions, making each visit unique.

The name honors our neighborhood's iconic black horse statue ("Kala Ghoda"), symbolizing our commitment to being a creative hub in Mumbai's historic art district.
