Our Story

Founded in 2012, Kala Ghoda Café quickly became a beloved institution in Mumbai's historic Fort district. What began as a small coffee shop has grown into a cultural hub where art, food, and community intersect.

Our philosophy is simple: source the best ingredients, prepare everything with care, and create a welcoming space for all. We partner with local farmers and artisans to bring you the freshest flavors Mumbai has to offer.

The café's name pays homage to the iconic Kala Ghoda art district we call home, and we continue this tradition by showcasing rotating exhibitions from local artists.
